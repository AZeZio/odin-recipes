Pizza

This is a delicious pizza made from ingredients such as pepperoni, olives, and green pepper. It's very easy to make, meaning just about anybody can make it!

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Olives
  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Green Pepper

Steps

  1. Knead the dough
  2. Put tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Put toppings on the dough
  4. Put in pizza oven for some time
  5. Take out and cut into 8 pieces before eating. Enjoy!
